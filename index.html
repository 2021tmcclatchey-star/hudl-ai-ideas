🔗 Shared Team Dashboards

Some of the most useful tools aren't just for one person — they're shared views that keep the whole team aligned. These are dashboards or trackers that multiple people on the team can access, update, and reference. Built once, used by everyone.

Team Account Health Board
One view showing all accounts across every CSM — last contact, tier, renewal date. Directors and CSMs see the same data simultaneously.
Pilot Program Tracker
Live tracker showing which clubs are in a pilot, their status, who's the CSM owner, and what's outstanding — visible to everyone working the program.
New Account Handoff Board
When an AE closes a deal, the CSM onboarding status becomes visible to the AE too — no more "where are we with this account?" Slack messages.
Club Issue Log
Shared tracker where any team member can log a camera issue, support escalation, or open item — so nothing falls through the cracks when someone's OOO.
